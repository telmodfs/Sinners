The place

Not just
a Bar

Since 2015, Sinner's Bar has been the Algarve's loudest secret. Tucked in Carvoeiro's R. dos Pescadores, this is the bar Fernando built for people who know what they want: heavy riffs, honest prices, company that actually gets it.

Iron Maiden. Black Sabbath. Metallica. The soundtrack Fernando built over a decade — not an algorithm, not a playlist. Just the real thing at the right volume. Cold beer, good darts, maybe a local medronho when the night runs long.

No pretension. No cover charge. No apologies. Come as you are.

What to expect

The Experience

01

The Music

Heavy metal and rock — pure and relentless. Iron Maiden, Sabbath, Metallica, Maiden, Slayer. Fernando's decade-built soundtrack. No algorithm. No compromise.

02

The Beer

Ice cold at prices that don't make you wince. Try the local medronho — Fernando's personal recommendation and one of the finest in the Algarve.

03

Fernando

The man behind the bar. Locals, expats, tourists — doesn't matter where you came from. You walked in. You're tribe now. Very friendly lad.

04

The Darts

Nice venue for darts, good selection of beers. The kind of bar where a simple Tuesday becomes a story you'll tell for years.

05

Late Night

Comes alive after dark. When the rest of Carvoeiro sleeps, Sinner's keeps going. A late-night bar that earns the name.

06

The Location

R. dos Pescadores 129. A short walk from the cliffs and the sea. Sin, then swim. That's the order of operations.
